How to Play

Click on the training buttons to improve your player's skills.

Each skill has a 10-second cooldown after training (reducible with upgrades).

You can only train up to 2 skills at the same time.

Use the Auto-Trainer Bot to train a random skill every 5 seconds for up to 10 minutes (upgradeable).

Earn Euros per training based on overall rating (0-25: 5€, 26-60: 10€, 61-100: 15€, 100+: 20€, increased by upgrades).

Use Euros to open different packs with varying costs and chances for items (Shirts, Shorts, Boots, Coaches).

Equip items for stat bonuses. Full set (3 items same country) multiplies bonuses by 1.5.

Your overall rating is a weighted average of all boosted skills based on selected position (or simple average if no position selected).

Play friendly matches (3-minute cooldown) to earn Euros (Win: 100€, Draw: 50€, Loss: 20€) and accumulate stats (Goals, Assists, Interventions, Saves).

Match stats depend on your position (e.g., only GKs get Saves). Scores are realistic based on ratings.

Equip a Coach to boost specific stats (e.g., Dylan for +100 Shooting).

Complete achievements to earn rewards and track your progress.

Purchase upgrades to reduce training cooldown, increase Euro earnings, or extend Auto-Trainer duration.

Progress, profile, inventory, currency, match history, stats, upgrades, and achievements are automatically saved to your browser.
